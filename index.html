🌙 Ivy's Silly Bedtime Story 🌙

One snowy night in Arendelle, Princess Ivy accidentally sneezed glitter snow all over Olaf. Suddenly Olaf became SUPER sparkly and started dancing like a silly chicken across the castle!

Elsa laughed so hard that tiny snowflakes flew out of her nose. Sven slipped on a banana peel, landed in a giant blueberry pie, and shouted, "Moooo!" even though he was a reindeer.

Ivy and Anna built a giant marshmallow mountain while Kristoff tried to climb it wearing fuzzy pink bunny slippers. Olaf rolled down the marshmallow mountain yelling, "I'M A SNOWBALL BURRITO!"

At bedtime, Elsa tucked Ivy into a magical fluffy blanket made from snow clouds. Olaf snored tiny snowflakes into the air while Sven wore sparkly pajamas covered in carrots.

And everyone in Arendelle whispered, "Goodnight Princess Ivy... sweet snowy dreams!" ❄️
